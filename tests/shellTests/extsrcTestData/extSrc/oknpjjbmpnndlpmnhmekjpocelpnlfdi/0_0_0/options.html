Send to Kindle

  1. On Amazon, go to the Manage Your Content and Devices screen.
  2. Under the Settings tab, scroll down to find the Personal Document Settings section.
  3. Add mercury-kindle@postlight.com to your Approved Personal Document E-mail List.
  4. Find the email address of your Kindle device under Send-to-Kindle E-Mail Settings and enter it below.
